Privacy Policy

Tradewind Labs LLC · Effective April 2026

Tradewind Labs LLC ("we", "us") operates Try Aloha and related messaging services. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

Phone numbers, message content, and timestamps for messages sent through our service. For business clients, business contact info, names, and service preferences.

How we use it

Solely to deliver the messaging services you've signed up for. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing.

Storage

Data is stored on encrypted servers (Cloudflare, Twilio, Anthropic). We retain message logs for 90 days for service quality and compliance, then delete them.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing taylor@tryaloha.ai. You can opt out of all messages by replying STOP to any text.

Carriers

Mobile carriers may also collect data per their own privacy policies.
